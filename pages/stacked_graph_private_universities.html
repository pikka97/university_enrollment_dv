    The count of the Universities
    grouped by income group

    In this page we show the number of the private universities groupped by region over the period 1950-2020.

    It shows the Americas, Europe/Oceania, and Asia demonstrating consistent growth in private institutions, while Africa exhibits a more gradual upward trend.

    In particular:

    • Americas: The Americas exhibit a steady increase in private universities, with the number almost doubling from around 300 in 1950 to over 500 in 2020.
    • Europe/Oceania: Europe/Oceania displays a similar pattern of growth, with the number of private universities more than doubling from around 700 in 1950 to over 1,800 in 2020.
    • Asia: Asia also experiences significant growth in private universities, with the number increasing from around 600 in 1950 to over 2,500 in 2020.
    • Africa: In contrast to the other regions, Africa's growth is more gradual, with the number of private universities increasing from around 200 in 1950 to over 500 in 2020.
